Essential Legal Protections Against Sabotage
Install the governance shield
before the trigger fires.
01
Buy-Sell Agreement
The "will" for the business. Without it, the spouse of a deceased partner becomes your new business partner — automatically. Defines trigger events: death, disability, fraud, divorce, voluntary departure.
Risk neutralized: Hostile ownership transfer
02
Right of First Refusal
Prevents an exiting knowledge anchor from selling their equity to a competitor or hostile third party. The business retains the absolute right to acquire first.
Risk neutralized: Competitive IP exposure
03
Deadlock Clause
Names a neutral third-party tiebreaker to resolve partner impasse within 30 days. Prevents operational paralysis when equity partners cannot agree on direction.
Risk neutralized: Decision paralysis
04
Account Owner Sovereignty
CEO/majority partner holds primary account owner status on all cloud environments — Caspio, AWS, Microsoft 365. The kill switch belongs to the principal. Always.
Risk neutralized: Internal sabotage via platform lockout
05
Lane Authority — Operating Agreement
Explicitly defines authority lanes: Sales, Product, Engineering, Finance. Prevents minority knowledge anchors from exercising hold-up power outside their designated lane.
Risk neutralized: Knowledge anchor hold-up power
06
Proof of Knowledge Protocol
All institutional logic documented via Forensic Interview. The departure of a 10% partner cannot cripple the 50% owner's environment. Every workflow sovereign.
Risk neutralized: Key-man dependency
